Frequently Asked Questions

What is Diet Fusion all about?

At Diet Fusion, we have personalised online diet programs for weight management & food caused diseases such as Thyroid, PCOS, Diabetes, Uric Acid, Liver & Cancer. Here, we believe in eating smart and right. Along with diet charts, clients learn to manage their weight & lifestyle.

How to book consultation appointment?

You can book an appointment directly by clicking on the Booking button or Register button and filling the form. If that doesn't work, you can either mail us at befitlookfab@gmail.com or fill the contact form on the website.

How often does the diet change?

One diet plan is to be followed for 10 days, during this period if you have any queries or issues you can tell your mentor on the chat section provided and she will address it within 8 working hours. So, in a 30 day program you get 3 diets, 60 day program you get 6 new diets, and in a 90 day program you get 9 new diets.

Do you give supplements?

BFLB is about pure natural foods that you eat and see around you. We don’t use any artificial powders, pills or anything which is not natural or locally available.

Do I need to exercise for weight loss?

We work on the 80/20 principle of nutrition science. For any weight loss/wellness/fitness program, diet plays 80% role. Once you get that right, you are bound to get results. If you can exercise and have time, we can recommend a suitable physical activity as well. In certain programs we recommend some physical activity; you should follow the advice of the mentor who will give you options that suit you.

Are the results guaranteed?

In any health care program, anywhere in the world, there will not be any guarantee. If you are dedicated, follow our diet plans & advice; RESULTS will follow. The key is to engage with the mentor for any difficulty as she will do all in her power to make it easy and explain things to you. If that be the case, results are sure.
