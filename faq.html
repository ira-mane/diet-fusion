Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Diet Fusion?

Diet Fusion is a premium wellness brand offering science-backed, customized nutrition plans and hormone-friendly meals to restore balance, energy, and health. We specialize in hormonal issues (thyroid, PCOS, menopause), gut health, metabolic disorders, and sustainable fat loss — without starving or extreme diets.

2. How is Diet Fusion different from other diet or meal services?

We don’t do cookie-cutter diets or generic salads. We combine:

Functional Nutrition + Ayurvedic principles
Hormone-friendly meals designed by experts
Premium, personalized plans for PCOS, thyroid, gut health, diabetes, and more
Subscription-based, chef-curated meals delivered fresh

We treat the root cause — not just symptoms.

3. Do you offer only diet plans, or meals too?

We offer both:

Personalized Diet Plans (3–12 month programs)
Cloud Kitchen Meals via subscription (available in Sakinaka & nearby areas)

Each is tailored to support your health condition, goals, and schedule.

4. I have PCOS/Thyroid/Gut issues. Will your diet work for me?

Yes. In fact, we specialize in these conditions.
Our approach reduces inflammation, balances hormones naturally, improves digestion, supports better cycles, and reduces stubborn fat.

5. What kind of clients do you work with?

We work with high-performing professionals, women with hormonal imbalances, corporate leaders, and busy individuals looking for sustainable results, not quick fixes. If you're ready to invest in long-term wellness, we’re for you.

6. Do you provide one-on-one support?

Yes. All premium plans include:

A personalized onboarding call
Regular progress check-ins
Direct support from our expert team via WhatsApp or email

This is not a DIY plan — we walk with you throughout.

7. How long will it take to see results?

Results vary, but most clients notice changes in:

7–14 days: Energy, bloating, mood
30 days: Digestion, inflammation, skin, weight loss
90+ days: Hormonal balance, metabolism reset, PCOS relief

This is a healing-first approach, not a crash diet.

8. Where do you deliver meals?

Our kitchen is based in Sakinaka, Mumbai, and we deliver to nearby premium locations.
For diet plans, we serve clients across India and internationally.

9. Is this plan safe for diabetes, cholesterol, or hypertension?

Yes. All our plans are medically reviewed and curated to manage chronic conditions safely.
We work alongside your doctor's advice and medications.

Meal Subscriptions: Starting at ₹150/- per meal

Diet Plans: Monthly, 3, 6, 9, and 12-month options
Custom pricing depends on goals, condition, and duration — we don’t do “one-size-fits-all” plans.

11. Will I have to give up my favourite foods forever?

Not at all. Our plans include smart swaps, cheat strategies, and even hormone-friendly desserts. We believe in balance — not restriction.

12. Can I lose weight even with thyroid/PCOS?

Absolutely. We’ve helped 100s of clients with PCOS, Hashimoto’s, insulin resistance, and more shed stubborn weight by targeting hormonal root causes, not just calories.

13. Is this program for men too?

Yes. We have specialized plans for male metabolic syndrome, testosterone support, gut reset, and fat loss without crash diets.
